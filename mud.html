System Messages

These come in a couple of flavors and in two styles... a block style where you can use them in your document flow or a fixed and centered on the top of your window area. They are animated to appear and disappear with a combination of jQuery and CSS3 animations.

Typography

All heading tags from 1 to 6. They have 20px margin at the bottom for a clean separation and no margin on the top so they align with things at the top.
